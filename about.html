About Our Parish

St. Peter The Apostle - Airport Parish has served our community with faith and devotion for decades. Our mission is to foster a welcoming, prayerful, and vibrant Catholic community rooted in the Gospel and sacramental life. We strive to be a place where all people can encounter Christ and grow in their spiritual journey.
